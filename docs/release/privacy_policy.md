# HabitLoop Privacy Policy

The full privacy policy is hosted at:
https://habit-loop-pearl.vercel.app/privacy-policy

## Key Declaration for Store Submission
- **Data Collection**: We do not collect any personal data.
- **Data Sharing**: We do not share any data.
- **Account Requirement**: We do not require or offer user accounts.
- **Storage**: All data is stored strictly locally on the user's device using standard local storage mechanisms. If the app is deleted, the data is permanently wiped.
- **Third-Party Services**: No analytics or tracking services are used.
